Earl's Bucket List includes:
*   Kayaking the entire Texas coast in one trip.
*   Kayaking a major Texas river from the last lake to the Gulf of Mexico (Done for Colorado and Brazos rivers).
*   Hiking the entire Texas coast in one trip.
*   Taking his sons sailing at night on the open ocean under a full moon.
*   Kayaking through Big Bend National Park.
*   Visiting his father's property in Honduras.
*   Hiking in Patagonia (Done).
*   Climbing Gallina Peak in New Mexico and leaving a gift (Done).
*   Climbing one Colorado "fourteener" per year with his boys (Done), including Mt Uncompahgre (5 times), Redcloud (4 times), Sunshine (1 time), Handes (2 times), and Mt Shavano (1 time).
*   Spending 100 nights under the stars over the course of one year (close with 74, still aiming for 100).
